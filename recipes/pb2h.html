PB2H Sandwich

You're my honey butter, baby.

Description

A variation on the classic peanut butter and jelly sandwich, for butter and honey lovers.

Implements

  • Butter knife
  • Honey dripper

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of sandwich bread
  • 1 dollop of peanut butter
  • 1 smear of unsalted butter
  • 1 drizzle of honey

Steps

  1. If whole grain, lightly toast the bread to soften
  2. Apply butter to slice A
  3. Apply peanut butter to slice B
  4. Drizzle honey liberally over slice B
  5. Place slices A and B together with peanut butter, butter, and honey facing inward to help avoid a mess while eating
  6. Enjoy before others notice!
